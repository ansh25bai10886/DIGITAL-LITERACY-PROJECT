Responsible Social Media Use Checklist
DO'S
1. Think before you post anything online
2. Keep your profile private and secure
3. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
4. Verify information before sharing (avoid fake news)
5. Be respectful and polite in comments and messages
DON'T
1. Don’t share personal information (address, phone number, passwords)
2. Don’t accept friend requests from strangers
3. Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
4. Don’t click on suspicious links or ads
5. Don’t spend excessive time on social media (avoid addiction)
