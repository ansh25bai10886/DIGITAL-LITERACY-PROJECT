 STAY SAFE ONLINE – A Simple Checklist (For College Students in India)

Staying safe online isn’t complicated—it’s about building a few smart habits in your daily digital life.

 **WHAT SHOULD YOU DO
1.Use strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) and don’t reuse the same one everywhere
2. Turn on 2-step verification (2FA) wherever possible—it adds an extra layer of safety
3. Check before you click—if a link looks strange or too good to be true, don’t open it
4. Stick to trusted apps and websites—download only from official stores like Play Store or App Store
5. Keep your phone and apps updated—updates fix security issues
6. Be careful on public Wi-Fi—avoid logging into bank or important accounts in cafés or public places
7. Share less personal info online—you don’t need to post everything publicly
8. Always log out from shared computers (like in labs or libraries)
**UPI &MONEY SAFETY 
1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP—no bank or app will ever ask for it
2. Don’t accept unknown payment requests (collect requests)—you could lose money instantly
 
**IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG
1. Report here: cybercrime.gov.in
2. Call immediately: 1930 (Cyber Crime Helpline)
